I am a geospatial machine learning engineer with a Ph.D. in Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing from Wuhan University and over a decade of industry experience spanning computer vision, 3D reconstruction, and autonomous-driving perception. My work has ranged from satellite and aerial 3D reconstruction to multi-sensor fusion localization, SLAM for AR devices, and large-scale image matching for domestic Chinese satellites.

The publications and patents below are from my doctoral research at Wuhan University on satellite and aerial imagery matching, bundle block adjustment, and sensor calibration.
